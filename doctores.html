We understand that the coronavirus (COVID-19) pandemic has put huge pressure on doctors and many of you have been working in very challenging circumstances for many months. Our guidance provides a framework for ethical decision making and you should continue to follow it as far as you can, using your professional judgement to apply it to the situations you face.

Dr.kisung

Doctors form an essential part of an effective response to the COVID-19 pandemic. We argue they have a duty to participate in pandemic response due to their special skills, reciprocal obligations in return. When reciprocal obligations are not met, doctors are further justified in opting out of specific tasks, as long as this is proportionate to the unmet obligation.
